How to setup the indicators
  • Enter your Trading View Username in the field on the Settings tab, click on dashboard “Get Trial” button
  • While the request is being processed, you will see a yellow “activating” status.
  • When access is granted, you will see a green “active” status
  • When your 3-days trial is expired, you will see a gray “expired” status
  • Trial period is granted only once during registration.

After you see the green “active” status, Add indicators to your favorites using links:

Scroll to the end of description and press the button “Add to Favorite indicators”
  • Remove the indicator from the chart.

  • Add the indicator again.
